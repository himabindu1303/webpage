Tips

Garlic

  1. The Fried Rice tastes great if the chopped Garlic is fried well but do not let the Garlic burn as the Rice becomes bitter.

Basmati Rice

  1. I have used Basmati Rice but you could use any other rice as well.

How to make Rice fluffy:

  1. Soak Rice for 30 minutes. Add 4 cups of Water, Salt and Oil and cook to 80% on a high flame.
  2. Cooking Rce to 80% means when you break a grain, it should still be a little solid. At that stage, strain the Rice and spread it on a plate coated with Oil or in a sieve and let it cool down fully. Then you get fluffy, non-sticky Rice.

Aromatic powder :

  1. I have used Aromatic powder found in the market instead of Ajinomoto. This is a substitute for Ajinomoto. In case you like Ajinomoto, you could use it or go without it

Ingridients

  • 2 tbsps Chopped Garlic
  • 3/4 cup Basmati Rice (Cooked in a fluffy manner)
  • 2 tbsps Oil
  • 1/4 Thinly Chopped Carrots
  • 1/4 cup Thinly Chopped Beans
  • Salt
  • 1/2 tsp White Pepper Powder
  • 1/2 Aromatic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Light Soya Sauce
